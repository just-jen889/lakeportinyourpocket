City of Lakeport
Small town. Big heart. Always here for you.
America's Oldest Lake
Clear Lake is 2.5 million years old — older than the Sierra Nevada. The oldest natural lake in North America is right here.
Bass Capital of the West
Clear Lake's record bass weighed 17.52 lbs, caught in 1990. Bassmaster Magazine ranks it the #1 bass lake on the West Coast.
